♠ Poker ♥

The first question people ask when you say you play poker is "Are you good?" Unlike other strategy games such as chess and backgammon, there is no global rating system to provide an objective measure of skill. Therefore, the only way to prove skill in poker is be profitable across a statistically significant sample size.

Sample Size and Volume

The easiest way to attain a large sample size is by playing multiple tables simultaneously online. I have been profitable across a modest sample size of 10,000 hands as shown in the graph below. To put this into context: 10,000 hands is approximately 500 hours of live poker and around 40 hours multi-tabling online.
