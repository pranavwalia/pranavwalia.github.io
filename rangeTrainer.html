Range Trainer

Ultimate poker pre-flop strategy trainer

Design, Study, Master

To be a successful Texas Hold'em player, it is essential to develop a strong pre-flop strategy. Without a complete strategy, it is impossible to make good decisions on later streets of the game. One of the greatest obstacles to becoming good at poker is the task of memorizing tons of scenarios for every possible pre-flop situation. This Software allows a user to enter their own ranges, save them to a markup file, and import them for quizzing. The goal is to make the process of mastering pre-flop oker strategy seamlesss, fast and easy.
